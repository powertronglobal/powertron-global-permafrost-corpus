To Determine Efficiency: 
 
Divide the measured kilowatts (kW) by the measured tons to obtain kW per ton. kW 
per ton is the industry standard to determine system efficiency.  
 
Example 
 
19.4 kW / 12 tons = 1.62 kilowatts per ton
