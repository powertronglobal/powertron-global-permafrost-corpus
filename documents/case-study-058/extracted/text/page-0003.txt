Hefei General Machinery Product
Inspection Institute
2
Under the inspection institute there are set up the 
following eight test rooms: the test room for R & 
AC and marine conditions, compressor test room, 
valve test room, seals test room, pump test room, 
packaging machinery test room, separator test 
room and heat exchanger test room. It possesses 62 
units (sets) of various types of inspection devices 
and can conduct total performance inspection and 
appraisal of such products as compressors, R & 
ACs (air conditioners, heating and cooling chillers, 
self-contained air conditioners, fans, fan-coil units, 
refrigeration compressors, refrigerators, 
refrigerated cabinets, condensing units and other 
equipment), valves and pipe fittings, mechanical 
seals, packing and static seals, pumps, packaging 
machinery (food packaging machinery), 
separating machinery, heat exchangers, 
environmental protection equipment and so on in 
accordance with both the national and 
international standards. The inspection institute 
has established and perfected the documented 
quality management system according to 
ISO/IEC17025 and ISO/IEC17020, etc. and 
requirements specified for special equipment and 
production license.
Besides, the inspection institute undertakes the 
following works: national quality supervision and 
spot-check of products, type test for 
administrative permit of special equipment and 
pressure pipeline components and evaluation of 
enterprise conditions, product inspection and 
examination of plant conditions for issuance of 
production license; undertakes product inspection 
for rating, etc. and product inspection and audit 
for national 3C certification, Fangyuan Mark 
certification, mechanical safety certification, 
energy conservation and energy efficiency 
certification, certification of CQC, GC, automobile 
products, environmental mark, etc.; inspection and 
audit work for certification of TUV, CE, etc., 
quality evaluation and appraisal of products for 
State key projects, quality control in the tendering 
and bidding process, arbitration and accident 
analysis concerning product quality.
The inspection institute will adhere to the working 
principle of scientificality, impartiality and 
independence, customer focus and continual 
improvement to continuously improve the testing 
and measuring level and ability and to provide 
customers with high quality service.
