costs associated with those savings. From an environmental impact standpoint, Caribbean Telecom Provider will also benefit all 
the surrounding communities in which they operate.  Imagine the positive environmental impacts of treating 
the rest of the Caribbean Telecom Provider portfolio of equipment spread across the equatorial regions of the world.
