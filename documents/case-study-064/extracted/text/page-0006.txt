Detailed Results for CRAC Units 1 & 2
System Description / Design Characteristics
These units provide critical cooling for the local Regional US Bank Data Center located on the 8th floor of the Regional US Bank 
facility located at 66 South Pearl Street, Albany, NY. Given the internal load from the Data Center year round, it 
is estimated that each of the units ran ~3,750 hours each per year prior to the ECM being implemented.
