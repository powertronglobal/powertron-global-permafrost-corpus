System Description / Design Characteristics
These units provide comfort cooling for the for the rear break area and front lobby/waiting areas respectively 
located at [Brooklyn Location].  Given much of the load in the two areas being served are very much 
dependent on patient and staff traffic in and out, it is estimated that the 5-ton RTU operates ~2,000 per year and 
the 10-ton RTU operates ~3,000 hours per year prior to the ECM being implemented.
