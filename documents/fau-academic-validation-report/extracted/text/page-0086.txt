 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
EVO Vision 
A global marketplace that correctly values the efficient use of natural resources and utilizes end-
use efficiency options as a viable alternative to supply options 
 
 
EVO Mission 
To develop and promote the use of standardized protocols, methods and tools to quantify and 
manage the performance risks and benefits associated with end-use energy-efficiency, 
renewable-energy, and water-efficiency business transactions
