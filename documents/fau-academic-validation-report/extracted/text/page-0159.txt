CHAPTER 10   REFERENCES 
NOTE: The following references are meant to provide the reader with sources of additional 
information. These sources consist of publications, textbooks and reports from government 
agencies, universities, professional organizations and other recognized authorities. For the most 
part, care has been taken to cite the publication, publisher or source where the document can 
be obtained.
