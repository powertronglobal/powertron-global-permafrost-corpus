Major Craft Retailer Saving More than just Energy at its Beltline Location
By treating the entire store and all it’s with PermaFrost NMR®, Major Craft Retailer will save approximately 105,300 kWh 
per year and all the costs associated with those savings. From an environmental impact standpoint, Major Craft Retailer is 
also beneﬁtting the surrounding community. 
The 105,300 kWh saved per year equates to the following:
           Prevention of 74.2 metric tons of CO2 emissions.
           Equivalent of removing annual greenhouse gas emissions from 15.4 passenger vehicles.
           CO2  emissions from the electricity use of 10.2 homes for a year.
           Greenhouse gas emissions avoided by recycling 27.8 tons of waste instead of sending it to a landﬁll.
           CO2 emissions from 8,328 gallons of gasoline consumed.
