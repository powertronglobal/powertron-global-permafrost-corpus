Global IT Services Provider Saving More Than Just Energy At Its Sloane Road Campus
By treating their chillers with PermaFrost NMR®, Global IT Services Provider has saved 104,000 kWh per year and all the 
costs associated with those savings. From an environmental impact standpoint, Global IT Services Provider is also beneﬁtting 
the surrounding community. 
The 104,000 kWh saved per year equates to the following:
           Prevention of 73.4 tonnes of CO2 emissions
           Equivalent of removing annual greenhouse gas emissions from 15.3 passenger vehicles
           CO2 emissions from the electricity use of 10.1 homes for a year
           Greenhouse gas emissions avoided by recycling 27.5 tonnes of waste instead of sending it to a landﬁll
           CO2 emissions from 37,366 liters of gasoline consumed
