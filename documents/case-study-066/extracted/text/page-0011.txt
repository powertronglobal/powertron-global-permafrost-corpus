Lobby RTU
Baseline kWh        10.22 kW * 4,380 Annual Operating Hrs = 44,764 kWh
Cost to Run Baseline        44,764 kWh * $.1331 = $5,958
Post ECM kWh        9.54 kW * 4,380 Annual Operating Hrs = 41,785 kWh
Revised kWh (based on avg. 33.4% restored capacity)        41,785 kWh * (1 - .334) = 27,829 kWh
Revised Cost to Run Post        27,829 kWh * $.1331 = $3,704
Avoided Energy Costs (Savings) Per Month        ($5,958 - $3,704) / 12 = $188
Cost of ECM        $1,625
ROI         $1,625 / $188 = 8.64 
 
  ROI = 8.6 months
