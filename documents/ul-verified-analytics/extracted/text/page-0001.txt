UL Veriﬁed In-Situ (in the ﬁeld) 
Veriﬁcation of M&V Process Summary
Powertron Global implements the International Performance Measurement & Veriﬁcation Protocols (IPMVP) 
framework utilizing the AHRI/ASHRAE standards in the ﬁeld to measure HVAC/R performance. Powertron 
Global brought Underwriters Laboratories (UL®) to audit and review the methodologies we employ in the ﬁeld 
to measure pre and post results. M&V was conducted on 4 separate DX systems in the New York City area.
